Privacy Policy

Welcome to Fikafy.

The Fikafy AB, Starrängsringen 8, 115 50 Stockholm, Sweden (hereinafter referred to as ‘Operator’) operates website (fikafy.com and other country specific domain fikafy.se) and our mobile apps (hereinafter referred as ‘Fikafy services’), under the name “Fikafy”. The Operator is the controller of personal data within the meaning of the General Data Protection Regulation(GDPR).

  1. Introduction

    The Operators understand and take data privacy seriously and want to stay transparent on how it deals with data.

    When Fikafy is used, the Operator may ask for a set of personal data, which means the information provided can be linked to a living person. Examples of personal data include name, email and address etc.. Below the Operator explains to the users of Fikafy (hereinafter referred to as ‘Users’ or User) how data, what kind of data and for what purpose data is collected and to whom this data may be shared with. And finally, the Operator informs users about their rights as well as internal rules for data storage.

  2. Data Collection and procession

    The Operator may collect data during registration of account and also while using Fikafy services.

    In order to give a clear idea about data, the personal data has been divided into three categories, which are based on how it is collected:

    1. Data that is given to Operator by user

      To create an account on Fikafy services, user personal information may be needed. The personal data includes Name, email, filing feedback form(if any) etc.

    2. Data collected when you use Fikafy services

      By using Fikafy services, the user provides information that is stored by the Operator in the database. That information is collected to ensure that the user is provided the services which are requested by the user. Operator may collect information when the user contacts Operators customer support (e.g. user email), so Operator can assist the user and answer queries. The Operator may use aggregated and anonymous data to improve its services.

      By using Fikafy services, the Operator may gather information about the user's activity, e.g. which screens and services are visited within Fikafy and information about how they are used. The collected data can be divided into the following types:

      1. Technical information

        Fikafy relies on Google Analytics and Firebase App Analytics, which records a user's device and internet connection, operating system, browser version, cookies and other unique identifiers. The information collected by Google and Firebase is usually processed as aggregated data in order to give a high level picture of Operator’s services used by its Users. The Operator may link such data to the User's account to improve that User's experience.

      2. Fikafy Services

        The Operator keeps record of User's account activities, such as logging in or out, invitation sent or received by other Users etc. The Operator stores information about a User's visit on Fikafy services, for which Operator mostly relies on Google and Firebase Analytics.

      3. Cookies and locally stored content

        When the user uses the Fikafy services the Operator may apply various technologies to recognise its User. Fikafy website uses cookies to facilitate use of the page, enable all functionalities and to improve Users experience. Fikafy relies on Google Analytics, which collects information, for instance, browser version, operating system, location etc., which helps Fikafy to provide and more optimised services to its Users.

  3. Purpose of processing the data

    Following are the purposes for which Operator uses Users data:

    1. Delivering the Fikafy services

      For the usage of Fikafy services, it is necessary that the Operator identifies the User, which allows Fikafy to show other Users located nearest to the User.

      Moreover, the Operator uses other personal information to personalise a User's account to enhance usage experience.

    2. Improvements in Fikafy services

      The Operator uses information to improve its services, for instance, making the registration, User search and sending invitation to Users as user-friendly as possible. And this may result in registering all events, e.g. clicks on buttons, screen views etc. and optimising Fikafy service based on that.

      Fikafy relies on Google Analytics and Firebase services to detect and analyse errors and improve user-experience.

    3. Personalised ads and recommendations

      The Operator uses information to personalise ads, for instance, using the Profession and showing relevant Users and relevant ads.

    4. Preventing, limiting and investigation abuse of Fikafy services

      The Operator uses information to restrict various forms of abuse of the Fikafy services. Such as spamming, unauthorised logins and all other actions that violate our Terms & Conditions or are prohibited by law.

  4. Legal basis for data procession

    Operator processes data for the performance and enhances user-experience of the Fikafy services, that could be showing relevant ads etc..

  5. Usage of Data by third parties

    Fikafy relies on Firebase for data storage & analytics and Google analytics for Fikafy website.

  6. Data Transfer

    The Operator will not share, sell, convey or otherwise disclose personal data other than as stated in this Privacy Policy, unless it is required to do so by law or the User has given her/his explicit consent to do so.

  7. Cookies and related technologies

    Fikafy website uses “cookies”, mainly “cookies” from Google analytics. Which helps Operator to improve its service and reach the right audience. Cookies can be disabled and removed from the browser at any time. Please note that in this case Fikafy services might be restricted to limited.

  8. User Rights

    The User has the right to be informed on if and what personal data of the User the Operator processes and to request a copy of such data (if it's processed). Users can send related queries to support@fikafy.com, Fikafy support will get back with answers/data as soon as possible.

    The User has the right to rectification, addition or deletion of personal data, where the data is inaccurate or the processing is unlawful.

  9. Duration of data storage

    Data is kept no longer than is necessary in order to keep Fikafy service upto date and smooth.
